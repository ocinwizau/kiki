FAQ

Honest answers


Do I need a website already?
Yes, ideally. Your AI assistant is a widget that sits on your existing website. If you don't have a website yet, we can still build your AI assistant and host it on a simple page for you — just mention it in the form.
How does it actually get on my website?
We give you one small piece of code to paste into your website. It works on Wix, Squarespace, WordPress, Shopify — any platform. If you're not sure how to paste it, we'll walk you through it step by step.
What if my hours or menu change?
Just send us a quick message — email, text, whatever suits you — and we'll update your AI within 48 hours. Updates are included in your monthly plan.
Is there a long-term contract?
No contracts at all. Pay month to month and cancel anytime with 30 days notice. The $199 setup fee is a one-time charge to build your assistant — after that it's $99/month with no strings attached.
What if the AI says something wrong?
Your AI only knows what we tell it — so it sticks to the facts about your business. If it gets a question it can't answer, it's trained to say "Give us a call on [your number] and we'll help!" — it never makes things up.
I'm not very tech savvy — is this for me?
Absolutely. You don't need to know anything technical. You fill in a form, we do the rest. The only thing you do is paste one line of code on your website — and we help you with that too if needed.
